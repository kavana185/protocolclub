About Us

Protocol Club is the official computer science club of BMS College of Engineering, Bengaluru.

We aim to create a community of tech enthusiasts and provide a platform for students to learn, collaborate and grow in the field of computer science.

From workshops and seminars to hackathons and coding competitions, we organize various events throughout the academic year to enhance the technical skills and knowledge of our members.
